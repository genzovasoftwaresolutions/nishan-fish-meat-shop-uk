Our Story

Nishan Fish and Meat

Welcome to Nishan Fish and Meat — your neighbourhood fish and meat shop on High Street, West Drayton. We are proud to offer the finest fresh fish and premium halal meat, with counters stocked daily for our local community.

From the day's catch to carefully selected steaks, poultry, and artisan cuts, every product is chosen for freshness, quality, and taste. Our team hand-prepares fillets and trims meat with care, so you always get the best for your table.

  • Sustainably sourced wild & farmed fish
  • Grass-fed & dry-aged Scotch beef
  • Free-range & corn-fed poultry
  • 100% halal meat from trusted suppliers
  • Fresh fish & meat ready to order
